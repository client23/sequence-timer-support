Privacy Policy for SequenceTimer

Effective date: 2026-04-03

Back to main page

SequenceTimer ("the App") is developed and published by Dominik Schmitt ("we", "us", or "our").

This Privacy Policy explains what data the App processes, how that data is used, and what choices you have.

Summary

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • Most data stays on your device.
  • If iCloud is enabled on your device, certain app settings and saved routines may sync through Apple's iCloud services.

Data We Process

SequenceTimer is designed to work primarily on-device. The App may process the following categories of data:

1. Data you create in the App

This may include:

  • saved timer routines
  • timer groups and timer steps
  • timer durations and sequence configuration
  • app settings such as language, sound options, screen-awake preference, Live Activities preference, and theme color
  • the current active timer state needed to restore your session

Purpose:

  • to provide the App's core functionality
  • to save your routines and settings
  • to restore your app state when you reopen the App

2. iCloud sync data

If iCloud is available and enabled for your device/account, the App may sync certain settings and saved routine data using NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore, an Apple-provided iCloud sync service.

This may include:

  • saved routines
  • timer configuration settings
  • theme color
  • selected limits and step size

Purpose:

  • to keep your app data consistent across your Apple devices

We do not operate our own servers for this sync data. Where iCloud sync is used, the data is handled by Apple under Apple's own terms and privacy practices.

3. Live Activities and lock screen display data

If Live Activities are enabled, the App may display current timer information on supported Apple system surfaces, such as the Lock Screen or Dynamic Island.

This may include:

  • current timer name
  • current timer progress
  • remaining time
  • routine context needed to display the active timer state

Purpose:

  • to show your active timer session in real time on supported system interfaces

This functionality uses Apple system frameworks and does not send that information to our own servers.

4. Audio-related processing

If you enable sounds or spoken countdowns, the App uses Apple audio system services on your device to play countdown audio and gong sounds.

Purpose:

  • to provide audible timer feedback

No audio recordings are collected, stored, or transmitted by us.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not knowingly collect or receive:

  • your name
  • email address through the App
  • phone number
  • precise location
  • contacts
  • photos
  • browsing history
  • health data
  • payment information
  • user-generated content uploaded to our servers

We also do not use the App to profile users for advertising or behavioral marketing.

Tracking

SequenceTimer does not track you across third-party apps, websites, or services.

The App's privacy manifest declares that tracking is disabled and that no data types are collected for tracking purposes.

Third-Party Services

The App does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

The App may rely on Apple platform services, including:

  • iCloud key-value storage
  • Live Activities / ActivityKit
  • Apple audio session services

These Apple services are governed by Apple's own terms, policies, and platform behavior.

Apple Privacy Policy:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Legal Basis and Purpose of Processing

Where applicable under privacy laws, we process data because it is necessary:

  • to provide the functionality of the App
  • to store and restore your settings and routines
  • to sync your app data across your devices when Apple iCloud sync is available and enabled
  • to display timer status via Apple system features you choose to use

Data Retention

Data stored locally in the App is retained until:

  • you change or delete it in the App
  • you delete the App from your device
  • your device or system settings remove the stored app data

Data synced through iCloud is retained according to Apple's iCloud storage behavior and your Apple account/device settings, unless you remove that data in the App or disable the relevant Apple sync features.

Your Choices

You can:

  • delete saved routines in the App
  • update or reload routines in the App
  • change or clear app settings in the App
  • disable Live Activities in the App settings
  • disable iCloud features at the Apple account/device level
  • delete the App to remove local app data from your device

Children's Privacy

SequenceTimer is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App.

International Users

If you use the App outside the country where the developer is based, your information may still be processed on your device and through Apple platform services according to Apple's infrastructure and policies.
